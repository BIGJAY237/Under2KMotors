About the House

Under2K Motors in motion

We bring the world's most coveted cars to discerning owners everywhere—no borders, no compromises.

Our story

From a boutique showroom to a global logistics operation, Under2K Motors invests in people first.

Founded by collectors and racing engineers, Under2K Motors began as a curated concierge that moved private collections across oceans. Today we help families, executives, and enthusiasts claim the car they dreamed of, backed by white-glove delivery teams and in-house experts.

We inspect every chassis, verify provenance, and orchestrate customs clearance so our clients can focus on the thrill of driving. Every shipment is insured and tracked, every purchase is transparent, and every guest receives dedicated support.
